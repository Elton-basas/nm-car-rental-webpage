N&M CAR RENTAL

Our Journey

Kevin Ferrer and Daisy Jane Ferrer are the owners of N&M Car Rental, located at 421 Concha St., Brgy 70, Tondo, Manila. N&M Car Rental operates 24 hours a day and is managed solely by the owners. Kevin handles the car deliveries, while Daisy Jane is the customer service representative.

Their journey began on March 27, 2021. Before starting their car rental business, they owned a thrift store. However, they noticed the rising demand for car rentals, where people rent cars for self-drive. Initially, they hesitated due to a lack of cars to start with.

Determined, they approached Toyota Cubao for a car installment plan. After submitting the required documents, they waited nearly three weeks for bank approval. They feared rejection due to the long wait, but on March 18, they received confirmation of approval. The next day, their first car was released, marking the beginning of their business.

Starting with just one car, N&M Car Rental has now expanded to six cars, catering to self-drive customers. Their success story is a testament to their dedication and commitment to providing quality rental services.

Frequently Asked Question

How do I book a car with N&M Car Rental?

You can book a car through our website, by calling us, or by visiting our rental location. Simply choose your desired vehicle, select your rental period, and provide the necessary details to confirm your booking.

What are the requirements to rent a car?

To rent a car, you must have a valid driver’s license, be at least [age requirement], and provide a valid credit or debit card for payment and security deposit.

Do you offer airport pickup and drop-off services?

Yes! We provide convenient airport pickup and drop-off services. Please inform us at the time of booking so we can arrange it for you.

Is there a mileage limit on rentals?

Some of our rentals include unlimited mileage, while others have a mileage cap. Please check the terms of your specific rental or contact us for details.

What is included in the rental price?

The rental price includes the base rental fee, standard insurance, and basic roadside assistance. Additional services such as GPS, child seats, or extra insurance coverage may come at an extra cost.
